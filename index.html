Why Choose Trézor Bridge™?

Trézor Bridge™ is a secure communication interface that connects your web browser to your Trézor® hardware wallet. Designed with privacy and performance in mind, Trézor Bridge™ replaces outdated browser extensions and ensures seamless crypto interactions. Whether you're sending assets, managing accounts, or verifying transactions, this lightweight software guarantees encryption integrity from end to end.

Unlike traditional browser extensions, Trézor Bridge™ works directly with your device through a local service, enhancing speed and minimizing attack vectors. It supports all major browsers and is updated frequently to ensure compatibility and stability across systems.

With Trézor Bridge™, your keys never leave the device. Every action is confirmed on the physical Trézor® screen, giving you full control and transparency over your crypto operations. The software is open-source, backed by an active community and the trusted development of SatoshiLabs.
